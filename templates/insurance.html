In-network benefits

Each therapist at Anchors of Hope Counseling Center is an independent contractor who chooses to partner with us. As such, each therapist makes the decision about which insurance providers (if any) to become panelled with. We know that for many, being able to use your insurance benefits is absolutely essential when seeking out mental health services. That is why, in the "Therapists" section of our homepage, we have listed under each individual therapist what insurances that therapist accepts. Please note that while we hope this way of highlighting what insurance each provider accepts makes finding the right therapist much easier, it is very important that you verify your insurance benefits prior to seeing any provider (whether at AOHCC or elsewhere).

For those wanting to utilize their Medicare benefits, please note that unfortunately none of our providers accepts Medicare.
